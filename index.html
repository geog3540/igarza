Bivariate Choropleth: Median Income and Unemployment Rate

Average Median Income and Unemployment Rate by Census Tracts of Los Angeles, California Data: [Census 2010] By looking at the map, it is clear that there is a pattern in the center of the city. Near downtown LA and towards the east side, there is a low median income rate and a high unemployment. Away from the city and into the suburbs of LA, there areas of high median income rates and low levels of enumployment. In both cases, a lot of the surrounding polygons are similar to one another in most cases.
